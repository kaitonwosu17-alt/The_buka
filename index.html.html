Good food. Great company.

The Buka started with one idea: Nigerians in Dublin shouldn't have to choose between doing it themselves and doing without.

Every pot is made the way it's supposed to be — smoky jollof, slow peppery soups, fish grilled properly, not rushed. We prepare fresh daily using quality ingredients, and we keep it authentic because that's the whole point.

We're open seven days a week for pickup and delivery across Dublin and the surrounding areas. Preordering is recommended, especially on weekends and match days, so we can have your food ready when you want it.
